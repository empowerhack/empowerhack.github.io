Our first hackathon will take place on the 28th/29th November in London.

The Hackathon will focus on preventing and reporting violence against women, supporting their access to essential services and enabling them towards social and economic empowerment. Hackers will choose a challenge faced by female refugees and work in teams to build a prototype technology solution.
