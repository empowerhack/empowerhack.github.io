Email Instructions

Follow the steps below to set up your EmpowerHack email.
If you have any issues, please contact us.
Other links: Apple Mail Instructions Configuration Tool
  1. Log in to Gmail
  2. Click on the gear icon in the upper right corner.
  3. Click on Settings.
  4. Under Accounts and Import tab, find Check mail from other accounts and click Add a POP3 mail account you own.
  5. Enter your full EmpowerHack email address and click Next step
  6. Fill in the details to match this screenshot. Pay particular attention to the port number.
  7. Optionally, click Next step so you can send from the account.
  8. Add the name you'd like to use when sending email
  9. Fill in the details according to the screenshot.
  10. All done! If forwarding is set up already, check your inbox for the verification code. If you can't find one, first check Spam and then contact us.
